Amazon Data Scientist Intern

Amazon

  • In, Bengaluru, Karnataka
  • Interview Base

Job Description

    We are seeking dedicated data scientists who possess strong leadership qualities and excel in the development, automation, and execution of analytical models for our systems. A solid educational and/or practical foundation in fields such as Computer Science, Engineering, Operations Research, or Process Control is highly valued for this role. Additionally, experience in the incorporation of model-based engineering tools and multidisciplinary analysis and optimization is considered an advantage.

Required Knowledge, Skills, and Responsibilities

  • Utilize data analysis and statistical methodologies to craft solutions aimed at enhancing the customer experience and providing valuable insights for strategic business decisions.
  • Identify key factors and root causes of business-related challenges and employ innovative approaches for forecasting and prediction.
  • Take charge of identifying, creating, overseeing, and implementing analyses that unearth potential areas for improvement, and communicate these findings through well-documented business recommendations.
  • Exercise leadership in cross-functional collaboration, guiding various teams in quantitative analysis, and ensuring that the solutions developed adhere to the highest standards of analytical rigor and data integrity.
  • Dive deep into business problems, addressing them at their core for effective and lasting solutions.

Education + Experience

  • Proven record of delivering impactful analytical solutions
  • skilled in R/SAS/Matlab
  • SQL, and Excel
  • Passion for problem-solving with big data
  • strong communication and fluent in English
